Hello!

I'm a Strategy Consultant at **Accenture** in Amsterdam, where I advise large-scale organisations on translating Data, Analytics, and AI into measurable business outcomes. I currently lead delivery on enterprise data and AI transformation programmes, working closely with C-level stakeholders on long-term strategy and roadmaps.

My background spans enterprise data strategy and governance, predictive analytics and forecasting, and AI-enabled transformation on €10M+ programmes. Before consulting, I led geospatial analytics teams at **Fugro**, where I built Python-based ML pipelines for critical infrastructure projects.

I hold an MSc in Applied Earth Sciences from **Delft University of Technology**, with a focus on Geoscience and Remote Sensing using applied data science. I'm currently completing the **AFP FP&A (FPAC)** certification and the **Claude Certified Architect – Foundations** certification.
